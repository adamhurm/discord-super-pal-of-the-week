Bringus Card Game

Collect cards of your server members. Trade. Upgrade. Flex.

How to play

Cards are awarded weekly in Discord. Run the command below to open your personal collection — the link is private to you and valid for 24 hours.

/my-collection

Rarities

Every card has a rarity. Collect 3 duplicates to trade in for a random card of the same rarity, or 5 to upgrade to the next tier.
