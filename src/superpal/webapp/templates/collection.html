Bringus Card Game — How to Play

Drawing Cards

Use /draw-card in Discord up to 5 times per week. The card posts publicly in the channel.

Super Pal of the Week holders get 10 draws per week. Resets every Sunday.

This Page

Run /my-collection in Discord to get a private link. The link works once — first click opens a 24-hour session. After it expires, run /my-collection again for a fresh link.

Cards you haven't drawn yet appear as ??? silhouettes — so you can see the full roster.

Trading In

When you own 3 or more copies of a card, a Trade In (3×) button appears on it. Click it to spend 3 copies and receive 1 random card of the same rarity.

Upgrading — /upgrade @member rarity

Spend 5 copies of the same [member + rarity] to receive that member at the next rarity tier.

  • Common → Uncommon → Rare → Legendary
  • Legendary cards cannot be upgraded further.
